## Company Summary

Chwezi Core Systems Limited is an integrated technology, business consulting, and security solutions firm headquartered in Kampala, Uganda. Established in December 2025, the firm delivers custom software development, enterprise systems, digital transformation, strategic consulting, and cybersecurity services to organisations across East Africa and beyond. Chwezi brings to market three proprietary SaaS platforms — Maduuka POS, Aqar Property Management, and Longhorn ERP — deployed across multiple countries, demonstrating end-to-end product delivery capability from concept through production support.
