## Proposal-Relevant Synthesis

- Premium proposal writing must sell confidence, not decoration.
- Every major claim should connect to mechanism, proof, risk control, and evaluator benefit.
- Executive summaries should lead with the evaluator's decision problem, not vendor biography.
- Cover letters should establish relevance, trust, and next-step confidence quickly.
- Case studies should show context, role, intervention, result, and relevance to the current bid.
- Premium pricing requires value logic, implementation assurance, credible differentiation, and clear trade-offs.
- SEO and AI-search guidance applies mainly to proposal-related public assets: blogs, capability pages, case studies, and thought leadership.
