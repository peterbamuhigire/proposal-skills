# Expert Pool and Scoring Model

Parent skill: [EAC E-Commerce BDS Programme Design](../SKILL.md).

## Expert-Pool Allocation Logic

| Step | Control |
|---|---|
| Demand estimate | Estimate days needed per diagnostic, TA design, coaching session, review, and reporting output. |
| Capacity check | Reconcile total demand to fee-days and calendar weeks. |
| Role fit | Assign tasks to the expert whose CV proves the capability. |
| Backstopping | Name who reviews outputs and covers absence. |
| Engagement risk | Define what happens when a selected company misses meetings or withholds data. |
| Quality gate | Every expert output passes evidence and QC review before sharing. |

## Proposal Scoring Discipline

For a two-page concept:

- Prioritise criteria with positive scoring weight.
- Use personnel-weighted criteria to guide CV evidence, not concept prose overload.
- Convert method claims into proof: tool, owner, cadence, output, review gate.
- Keep implementation plan realistic against fee-days and phase duration.

## Engagement Tactics

- Onboarding call with attendance expectations.
- Diagnostic data request with deadline and confidentiality note.
- Short written company action plan after each major interaction.
- Missed-session escalation ladder.
- Visible wins in the first 30 days to keep founders engaged.
