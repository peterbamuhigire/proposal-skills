# Accounting Engine Positioning

## Why It Matters

Most operating systems create transactions first and ask finance to reconcile later. The proposed architecture removes that gap: the operational event and the accounting consequence are one controlled workflow.

## Client Benefits

- No duplicate data entry into separate accounting software for routine books.
- Faster month-end close because journals are produced as work happens.
- Better audit trail because every journal links to the source document, actor, timestamp, and posting rule.
- Management reports and financial statements agree because they read the same ledger.
- Branch, project, donor, product, and department reporting become dimensions of the same accounting record.

## Differentiator

This is not an integration-only approach. Integrations can still exist for tax filing, bank feeds, payment providers, or external audit exports, but the ledger of record lives inside the system being built.
