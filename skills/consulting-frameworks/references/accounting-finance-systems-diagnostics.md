# Accounting and Finance Systems Diagnostics

Use this reference when a proposal concerns bookkeeping improvement, ERP finance, accounting software, POS-to-accounting integration, school fees, healthcare billing, inventory accounting, payroll, tax compliance, finance dashboards, Excel models, or management accounts.

## Diagnostic Domains

| Domain | What to assess | Typical evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Record to report | Chart of accounts, journals, period close, trial balance, management accounts, statutory statements | GL export, TB, monthly accounts, close checklist |
| Source documents | Invoices, receipts, bills, POs, delivery notes, payroll records, bank/mobile money statements | Document samples, filing structure, system exports |
| AR and revenue | Invoicing, receipts, credit notes, allocations, aging, bad debt, revenue recognition | Customer ledger, receipt book, aging report, contracts |
| AP and procurement | Supplier bills, payment approval, duplicate prevention, PO/receipt matching, aging | Supplier ledger, bills, payment batches, PO records |
| Cash and bank | Cashbook, bank/mobile money reconciliation, cash counts, petty cash, payment controls | Bank statements, MoMo statements, cashup sheets |
| Inventory accounting | Stock valuation, COGS, shrinkage, stock count variance, write-offs, slow stock | Stock ledger, count sheets, item costs, adjustment logs |
| Fixed assets | Asset register, depreciation, disposals, custody, impairment/revaluation | Asset list, invoices, depreciation schedule, disposal approvals |
| Tax and statutory | VAT, PAYE, WHT, NSSF, duties, tax point, return evidence, EFRIS where relevant | Returns, tax schedules, payroll reports, URA evidence |
| Management accounting | Cost centres, profit centres, project costs, budgets, variance, contribution margin | Budgets, cost reports, branch/project P&L |
| Systems and controls | Posting rules, roles, approvals, audit logs, period locks, reports, integrations | ERP/POS screenshots, role matrix, audit logs, workflow config |
| Excel model quality | Inputs, calculations, checks, formulas, scenario logic, version control | Workbooks, formula review, check sheets |

## Methodology Building Blocks

### Accounting Current-State Review

- Map source transaction to ledger flow for sales, purchases, payments, payroll, inventory, fixed assets, tax, and adjustments.
- Reconcile AR, AP, inventory, fixed assets, bank/mobile money, tax, and payroll schedules to the general ledger or available books.
- Review chart of accounts for reporting usefulness, statutory needs, and management dimensions.
- Test whether debits equal credits, trial balance maps cleanly to statements, and reports can be regenerated from source data.

### Controls and Governance Review

- Assess segregation of duties for invoice creation, receipt, payment, supplier bank changes, journals, write-offs, stock adjustments, payroll, and period close.
- Review approval thresholds, maker-checker workflows, closed-period controls, audit logs, reversal procedures, and evidence retention.
- Identify direct posting to control accounts, unallocated receipts/payments, suspense balances, stale reconciling items, and manual spreadsheet overrides.

### Cost and Management Accounting Review

- Separate fixed/variable, direct/indirect, controllable/non-controllable, and one-time/recurring cost logic.
- Review cost centre, profit centre, branch, project, product, or service-line reporting.
- Where operations are repeatable, assess standard costing, budget variance, flexible budget, contribution margin, break-even, and overhead allocation.
- Identify where product/customer/project profitability is distorted by weak cost allocation.

### Systems and Data Review

- Assess ERP/POS/accounting software configuration: chart of accounts, fiscal periods, posting rules, tax codes, document types, number ranges, open items, reconciliation accounts, and reporting dimensions.
- Review integration gaps between POS, inventory, billing, payroll, bank/mobile money, tax, and accounting.
- Evaluate Excel models for formula risk, missing checks, hard-coded outputs, broken links, and weak scenario logic.

## Proposal Language

"Our finance diagnostic will trace transactions from source documents through subledgers, reconciliations, control accounts, management reports, and statutory outputs. This allows us to identify whether finance weaknesses arise from poor bookkeeping discipline, weak chart-of-accounts design, missing reconciliations, ERP configuration gaps, spreadsheet model risk, tax exposure, or inadequate management accounting."

## Deliverable Options

- Accounting and bookkeeping diagnostic report
- ERP finance configuration and controls assessment
- Chart of accounts and reporting-dimension redesign
- AR/AP, bank, inventory, fixed asset, payroll, and tax reconciliation review
- Finance process and controls matrix
- Management accounting and cost-centre reporting design
- Excel financial model audit and rebuild plan
- Finance dashboard and KPI specification
- Month-end close checklist and governance pack
