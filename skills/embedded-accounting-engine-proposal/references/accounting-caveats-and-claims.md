# Accounting Caveats And Claims

## Claims Allowed When In Scope

- The system replaces routine bookkeeping workflows.
- The system produces audit-ready financial statements and supporting schedules.
- The system reduces dependency on separate accounting subscriptions.
- The system gives accountants and auditors drill-down from report to journal to source document.

## Claims Not Allowed Without Explicit Scope

- The system replaces statutory audit.
- The system replaces licensed tax advice.
- The system makes complex IFRS judgements automatically.
- The system guarantees tax compliance without current-rate verification and jurisdiction review.

## Standard Caveat

The system will perform bookkeeping, posting, reconciliation, and report generation according to the configured accounting policies and approved posting rules. Where law, regulation, donor conditions, or accounting standards require professional judgement or formal sign-off, the client remains responsible for obtaining the appropriate accountant, auditor, tax, or legal review.
